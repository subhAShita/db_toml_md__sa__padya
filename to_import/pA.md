<details><summary>Text</summary>

पाषाणे पतितं बीजं सपक्षं न विवर्धते ।  
जडानां मध्यगस् तद्वन् मेधावी न विराजते ॥
</details>

<details><summary>मानसतरङ्गिणीकृत्</summary>

Even as a winged seed that has fallen on a rock does not sprout  
So also an intelligent man in the midst of the dim-witted fails to shine.
</details>
