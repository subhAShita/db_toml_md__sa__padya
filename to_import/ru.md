<details><summary>Text</summary>

रुक्षे दृषत्पृष्ठगतं सपक्षं  
बीजं प्ररोहाय न कल्पते हि ।  
तथा जडानां निकरे निमग्नः  
प्राज्ञो न लोके प्रथितो विभाति ॥
</details>

<details><summary>मानसतरङ्गिणीकृत्</summary>

A winged seed that has come to rest upon the surface of a dry rock  
is indeed incapable of sprouting.  
Likewise, a wise man immersed [in the company] of a multitude of dullards  
does not shine forth as one of renown in the world.
</details>
